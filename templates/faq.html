Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ConnectNet

Account & Profile

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation, fill in your details, and follow the verification process.

Can I change my username?

Yes, you can change your username once every 30 days from your profile settings.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Networking

How do I connect with other students?

Use the Network page to find students, filter by major or year, and send connection requests.

Can I message someone without connecting?

No, you need to be connected to send messages. This helps maintain a safe and trusted community.

How do I manage my connections?

Visit your profile's "Connections" tab to view, manage, and organize your network.

Events

How do I create an event?

Click "Create Event" on the Events page, fill in the details, and publish your event.

Can I edit or cancel an event?

Yes, event organizers can edit or cancel events from the event management page.

How do I get event reminders?

When you RSVP to an event, you'll automatically receive email and in-app reminders.

Privacy & Security

Is my information private?

Yes, you control your privacy settings. Visit your profile settings to manage what others can see.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Use the report button (flag icon) on any post or message, or contact our support team.

Can I block other users?

Yes, you can block users from their profile or through the messaging interface.
